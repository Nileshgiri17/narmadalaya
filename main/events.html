School Building Inauguration  

8 Feb, 2020  

New School Building Inauguration

November 2018 brought us a big pleasant surprise. General Insurance Corporation of India (GIC Re) granted us funds for construction of the first and second floor of the school building of Ramkrishna Sarda Niketan, Lepa Punarvas.
The school building was inaugurated on 08 Feb 2020 by Mr. G. Radhakrishna, Deputy General Manager of General Insurance Corporation of India (GIC Re). Ms. Rani Durve General Manager of Reserve Bank of India presided over the program. Ms. Sunanda Bhosekar, retired Assistant General Manager of RBI, Mumbai and Dr. Lalita Deshpande, President N.A.R.M.A.D.A. graced the occasion.

Narmada Jayanti Utsav 2020  

1 Jan, 2020 to 2 Feb, 2020  

Narmada Jayanti Utsav 2020

Narmada Jayanti Utsav was celebrated from 31 January to 02 February 2020. Honourable Ex Speaker of Loksabha Smt Sumitra Tai Mahajan inaugurated the program. Smt Kalyani Namjoshi, Pune spoke on River Culture. Mrs Dhanashri Lele from Thane also spoke on river culture and Sant Kabeer. Mrs Anagha Chiplunkar, Nasik spoke on Narmada Parikrama and Bhagvad Geeta. Mr Duttaji Chitale from Pune presented a musical program of Geet Ramayana.
