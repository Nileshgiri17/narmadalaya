NARMADA SWAR LAHRI

Musical programs by our students -
  1. Sanskrit stotra by Shri Adi Shankaracharya.
  2. Sanskrit stotra by Shri Vasudevanand Saraswati
  3. Narmada pradushan – awareness songs
  4. Hindi text book poems - orchestra
  5. Nimadi and Bhilala folk songs and dances
Narmada Swar Lahri is a musical orchestra of students of Ramkrishna Sarda Niketan. They are trained by well-known classical singer Mrs. Shubhada Marathe. Our musical troupe ‘Narmada Swar Lahri’ presented their programs at Reserve Bank of India, Mumbai, Bharatiya Vidya Bhavan, Ramkrishna Sarda Math- Pune, Rally for Rivers- Indore, River Festival- Hoshangabad, Vaidikvidya Peetham- Chichot etc so far.
