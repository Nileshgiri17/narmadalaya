AKRUTI - NARMADA

(A Solar Dryer production unit)

In Collaboration with Bhabha Atomic Research Centre – Rural Technology Department

  1. First Knowledge Partner of Bhabha Atomic Research Centre in Madhya Pradesh in Rural Technology. It was inaugurated by veteran scientist Padma Vibhushan Dr. Anil Kakodkar.
  2. Production of Solar Dryers.
  3. Training in dehydration of vegetables and fruits.
  4. Soil Testing.
  5. Participation in International/National Conferences by our students.
A rural technology centre has been set up in collaboration With Bhabha Atomic Research Centre, Mumbai. The agreement of collaboration was signed with their Rural Technology Department in April 2017.
• We have started production of Solar Dryers and so far sold them in Nagaland, Gujarat and Maharashtra Market.
• We have also started production of dried onion, kasuri methi, herbal tea, tomato powder and spinach powder.
• Our fabrication unit has been successful in erection of solar panel frames and huge shades for Goshala and vehicle parking.
• This year, all the furniture required for the three formal schools was made by our senior students.
• We have started making wooden trays out of the scrap left in furniture making. This new product has received tremendous response from customers.
