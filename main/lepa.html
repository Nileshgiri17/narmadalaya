Ramkrishna Sarda Niketan


Affiliated to State Education Board of Madhya Pradesh

Dise Code : 0987654321
Registration Number : 0987654321

Ramkrishna Sarda Niketan, formal school started at Lepa Punarvas to impart quality specially for the education if the children of economically weaker section of the society in April 2015. Branches of Ramkrishna Sarda Niketan were opened at Bhattyan village in April 2016 and Chhoti Khargone village in June 2016.

All the facility including Education, Vocational skills, Mid day meals, School uniform etc. are provided totally free of cost.

Students are proving their talents in Music, Sports, Vocational skills and Academics as well.

Teacher's Training

“Regular teacher’s training is conducted at Ramkrishna Sarda Niketan. With the help of resource persons from Mumbai, Pune, Nasik etc., these training camps are regularly organized. So far Mrs.Rati Bhosekar, Smt. Renu Gavaskar and Ms. Kavita Bhagwat etc. Renowned educationists from Thane and Pune conducted training specially for pre-primary teachers. Shri Aniruddha Patil, Shri Rupesh Nerkar and Chaitali Nerkar conducted workshops on science in day to day life. Dr. Lalita Deshpande, a renowned mathematician conducted training programs for the teachers. Volunteers of Swaroop Wardhini, Pune conducted maths/science workshops twice during the year for teachers and students. Ms. Bharati Thakur conducted weekly traing program for English and Hindi teachers.”

All these training programs were well appreciated by students and teachers.
